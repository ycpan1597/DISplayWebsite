Erin Ciliv

Co-designer

Erin Ciliv is a 20 year old student at the Evergreen Transition Program, Class of 2018. Erin was born with Cerebral Palsy and uses a wheelchair to get around. She is excited to be a part of the team at UW, helping to make games easier to play for kids with all types of disabilities. In her free time, Erin loves to swim, play the drums, listen to music, and spend time with friends. Erin is very proud of her work on this project. It is important to her to help other kids with disabilities feel included, because she knows what it’s like to be in their shoes. Erin’s motto in life is “disability is not inability,” and is excited for her future because she knows she will accomplish great things.
