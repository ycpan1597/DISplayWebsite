What's AOI?

AOI stands for "Accomplishments, Objectives, and Issues." Teams present their AOI during the beginning of each class as an update on their project. The goal is to ensure that all groups feel knowledgeable about other teams' progress and draw on the collective expertise of the class to solve possible issues.
